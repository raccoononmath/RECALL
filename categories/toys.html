December 10, 2024 Toys Recall #24-312

Skip Hop Recalls Activity Centers Due to Choking Hazard

By Skip Hop Inc. (Carter's subsidiary)

⚠️ Hazard: Small parts can break off from the toy attachments on the activity center, posing a choking hazard to young children.

🔧 Remedy: Consumers should immediately remove the toy attachments and contact Skip Hop for free replacement toys.

Skip Hop Explore & More Baby Activity Center

3-stage activity center for babies 4 months and older. Model numbers 303325 and 303326.

Units: 53,000
