March 15, 2025 Strollers Recall #25-051

Graco Recalls Lightweight Strollers Due to Fall Hazard

By Graco Children's Products Inc.

⚠️ Hazard: The stroller's hinge mechanism can partially release during folding and unfolding, posing a fall hazard to children in the stroller.

🔧 Remedy: Consumers should immediately stop using the recalled strollers and contact Graco for a free repair kit.

Graco NimbleLite Stroller

Lightweight folding stroller with canopy and storage basket. Model numbers GL2024A and GL2024B printed on a label on the stroller frame.

Units: 85,000
