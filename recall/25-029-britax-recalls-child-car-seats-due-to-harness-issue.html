RECALLED February 05, 2025 Recall #25-029

Britax Recalls Child Car Seats Due to Harness Issue

Product Details

Britax Boulevard ClickTight Convertible Car Seat

Convertible car seat for children 5 to 65 pounds. Model numbers E1C199T and E1C199V.

Units: 110,000

Hazard

The harness chest clip can crack or break during normal use, which could prevent the harness from properly restraining the child in a crash.

Remedy

Consumers should contact Britax for a free replacement chest clip. Continue to use the car seat with the harness until the replacement clip arrives.
