RECALLED January 15, 2025 Recall #25-018

Target Recalls Cat & Jack Children's Pajamas Due to Burn Hazard

Product Details

Cat & Jack Fleece Pajama Sets

Children's two-piece fleece pajama sets in sizes 4-12. UPC numbers starting with 492000.

Units: 65,000

Hazard

The children's pajamas fail to meet the federal flammability standards for children's sleepwear, posing a burn hazard to children.

Remedy

Consumers should immediately stop using the recalled pajamas and return them to any Target store for a full refund.
