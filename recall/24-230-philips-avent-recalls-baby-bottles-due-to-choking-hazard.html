Philips Avent Recalls Baby Bottles Due to Choking Hazard

Product Details

Philips Avent Anti-Colic Baby Bottles

4oz and 9oz baby bottles with AirFree vent. Batch codes starting with AA23 printed on the bottom.

Units: 320,000

Hazard

The anti-colic insert valve can detach from the bottle during feeding, posing a choking hazard to infants.

Remedy

Consumers should immediately stop using the bottles and contact Philips for free replacement bottles.
