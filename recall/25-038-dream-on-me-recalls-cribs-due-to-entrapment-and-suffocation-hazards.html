RECALLED February 20, 2025 Recall #25-038

Dream On Me Recalls Cribs Due to Entrapment and Suffocation Hazards

Product Details

Dream On Me Synergy 5-in-1 Convertible Crib

Convertible wood crib that can be used as a toddler bed, daybed, and full-size headboard. Model numbers 657-N, 657-W, 657-E.

Units: 28,000

Hazard

The crib slats can loosen and detach, creating gaps that pose entrapment and suffocation hazards to infants.

Remedy

Consumers should immediately stop using the recalled cribs and contact Dream On Me for a full refund or free replacement crib.
