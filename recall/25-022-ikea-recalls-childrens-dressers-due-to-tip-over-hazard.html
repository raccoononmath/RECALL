IKEA Recalls Children's Dressers Due to Tip-Over Hazard

Product Details

IKEA KULLEN 3-Drawer Dresser

Three-drawer dresser sold in white and birch effect. Article numbers 503.092.45 and 803.092.44.

Units: 200,000

Hazard

The dressers pose a serious tip-over and entrapment hazard if they are not properly anchored to the wall, which can result in death or injuries to children.

Remedy

Consumers who have not anchored the dresser should immediately place it in an area children cannot access and contact IKEA for a free wall-anchoring kit.
